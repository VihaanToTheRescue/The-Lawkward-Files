About The Lawkward Files

Dive into The Lawkward Files, a rollicking 15-story legal satire series that’s madder than a Delhi traffic jam during Diwali! Every set of five stories dances to its own quirky theme, serving up a spicy mix of Indian chaos, courtroom hungama, and objects behaving like filmi villains. The first five tales, the Quirky Courtroom Chronicles, unfold in the Ludicrous Bench, where small-town judges tangle with naughty clocks, thieving sandals, and rebellious fans, poking fun at India’s love for drama over chai. Stories 6 to 10, the Corporate Court Capers, shift to the glitzy Gilded Gavel Court, roasting startup mania as pens plot espionage, laptops stage coups, and coffee machines brew sabotage, all with a side of X trend tamasha. Stories 11 to 14, the Village Verdict Vagaries, bring rural madness to the Panchayat of Peculiarities, where wells trespass, mango trees slander, and tractors mutiny, capturing the gossip-fueled heart of India’s villages. Then comes the grand finale, story 15, The Great Guffaw Gambit, a 4,000-word explosion in the Supreme Court of Silliness, where every character, from buffalo-obsessed judges to lassi-sipping lawyers, and every object, from ticking clocks to creaking branches, collides in a riotous battle over a bonkers laughter ban, tying all themes into a masala-packed climax that’s pure India!
